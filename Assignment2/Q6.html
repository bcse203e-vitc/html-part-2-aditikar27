Classic Pancake Recipe


Ingredients:

  • 2 cups of flour
  • 2 eggs
  • 1.5 cups of milk
  • 1 tsp of sugar
  • 1 tsp of baking powder

Steps:

  1. Mix all the dry ingredients in a bowl.
  2. Whisk the eggs and milk together, then combine with the dry mixture.
  3. Heat a pan and pour a ladle of batter.
  4. Cook until bubbles form, then flip and cook the other side.
  5. Serve with syrup or toppings of choice.

Nutrition Facts

Calories: 210 per serving, Protein: 6g, Carbs: 40g, Fat: 5g
